My experience as an exchange student at Boise State University

My name is Sofie Eriksen. I am 23 years old and I am from Denmark. I am currently finishing my last semster of my bachelor's degree in media studies. During the spring semester 2023 I have been studying abroad as an exchange student at Boise State University. This blog will take you through my journey.

This blog serves as my final project in the Web Design class that I am taking. At Aarhus University I have developed strong analytizal skills but I have not been trained in any practical skills. As a media studies major I am certain that web design will only become more relevant and this is why I was happy to enroll in this course. I really wanted to learn some new skills where my progress actually can be tracked and seen visually. During this course I have become familiar with the basics of web design. I have both learned how to code HTML and CSS and I have also gotten experience with setting up a website using non-coding platforms such as Wordpress and Squarespace.

I am grateful that I have been able to get new ecperiences in many different ways during my four months in the United States. I have developed new academic skills, I have met new friends and I have travelled to new placed during my stay here. Keep on reading to find out more about this.
